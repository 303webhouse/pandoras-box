## Why this needed prep (correction to the original brief)
The brief assumed ~3,900 UW calls/day at the full 40-ticker list (1 call/ticker).
**It actually makes 2 UW calls per ticker** — `get_flow_per_expiry`
(`/flow-per-expiry`) PLUS `get_snapshot` (`/stock-state`). Real cost ≈ double, so
a naive trim-to-20 lands too close to the cap. See the envelope.

## Governor status (verified in integrations/uw_api.py)
- Token-bucket limiter (60/min burst, set in the 06-16 rework), circuit breaker,
  retry-with-backoff, and a `UWUnavailable(RATE_LIMITED)` sentinel on 429 —
  **429s are NOT retried** (no retry storms). The poller inherits all of this
  automatically via `_uw_request`. Satisfies AEGIS's conditions.
- Daily-budget counter with alerts at 50/70/85/90% of 20K. **BUT the per-caller
  daily quota gate (B2) is in OBSERVE mode — it logs, it does not block.** So the
  per-minute ceiling is hard-enforced; the per-day cap is alert-only. If the
  poller eats the day's headroom, other UW consumers degrade to `UWUnavailable`
  (graceful — but that is exactly the 06-16 failure pattern).

## Recommended configuration
**Trim to LIQUID_UNIVERSE (20) AND drop the snapshot enrichment call (flow-only).**
Keeps 5-min freshness (needed for intraday SPY scalping) and cuts a flaky,
non-essential `/stock-state` call. Lands ~90% with real headroom vs ~98%.
