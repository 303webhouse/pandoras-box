## Equity-specific sub-rules that don't cleanly apply (shared footnote, per Phase 0.2)

None of the seven strategies above use equity sector-rotation or PYTHIA VA-location signals — full sub-rule-by-sub-rule mapping is in `s4-phase0-findings.md` §0.2. Summary: **Sector-Rotation Regime Spec does not apply** (crypto has no GICS-style sectors; the identified analog — BTC dominance/ETH-BTC/alt-breadth — is named in the committee brief but unbuilt, `alt_gate.status="NOT_AVAILABLE"`). **PYTHIA's Location-Quality Multiplier applies differently and only partially** — POC/VAH/VAL exist and CVD_ABSORPTION's structural-level anchor is conceptually the closest thing to a location-quality signal in this population, but it's a trigger-location gate, not the equity rule's +0.5/−0.5 numeric grade modifier, and crypto's 3-rolling-session structure makes "prior-session developing VA" ambiguous in a way that's genuinely unresolved, not just unported.
